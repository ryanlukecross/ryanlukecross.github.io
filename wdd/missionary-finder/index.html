Find the Missionaries that Baptized You!

        Our purpose is to facilitate the reunion of former missionaries of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day saints to the people whom they had a chance to teach the gospel. Our website is designed to take into consideration times, locations and specific events of converts and missionaries and to link them together and give them a platform to once again be reunited. Feel free to login or sign up to start searching today. For those of you with questions, please visit our FAQ section.
