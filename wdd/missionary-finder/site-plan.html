Overview

Purpose

I am a former missionary. I interacted with many converts while on my mission. Many of these converts couldn’t tell you recent information about the missionaries that taught them. For most of them it was because they were baptized a while ago and haven’t taken time to get onto social media. Our purpose is to give you an opportunity to find the missionaries who taught you the gospel and for them to have a platform to find you too!

Audience

While anyone is welcome to use our website to find the missionaries that taught them the gospel, our website is tailored for people who don't have access to social media accounts and who haven't seen or heard from their 'Missionaries' in a long time.
